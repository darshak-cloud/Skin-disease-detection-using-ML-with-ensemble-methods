About Skin Diseases

Skin diseases can affect anyone, regardless of age or gender. Some common skin conditions include eczema, psoriasis, and acne. Early detection and proper treatment are crucial in managing these conditions.

Eczema

Eczema causes the skin to become itchy, red, and inflamed. It is most common in children but can occur at any age.

Psoriasis

Psoriasis speeds up the life cycle of skin cells, causing them to build up rapidly on the skin's surface.

Acne

Acne occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells, causing pimples.
